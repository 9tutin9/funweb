Process

How we work together

Transparently, step by step. Without good communication it doesn't work – I continuously share work, ask for details and we fine-tune everything together.

Initial consultation

We'll go through your goals, budget and priority outputs (website, emailing, database, graphics). I'll recommend a suitable platform (Webflow, Shoptet, WordPress).

  • Short call / meeting (30–45 min)
  • Writing down requirements and expectations
  • Preliminary time and budget estimate
Output: clear goal and scope

Design and plan

I'll prepare the website/content structure, wireframes and milestone plan. For tech projects, integration specifications (API, database, CRM).

  • Information architecture & wireframe
  • Roadmap with milestones and deadlines
  • Technical requirements and approaches
Output: approved plan

Implementation

I create the website/graphics/database and continuously share samples. I welcome short feedback so work flows without hiccups.

  • Continuous previews (shared links)
  • Quick adjustments based on feedback
  • Work in iterations (sprints)
Output: functional prototype/samples

Communication & feedback

Without communication it doesn't work. We'll agree on a rhythm (e.g. 1× per week short sync) and clear channels (email, Slack, Trello).

  • Continuous status without fluff
  • Quick decisions based on samples
  • Transparent overview of tasks and deadlines
Output: no surprises

Delivery & launch

Finalization, testing and deployment. I'll hand over access and a short manual, possibly train the team.

  • Final quality check (speed, SEO basics, security)
  • Access rights & documentation
  • Pre-launch checklist
Output: project ready to use

Support & development

I offer long-term management, optimization and marketing (campaigns, emailing, automation), according to what makes business sense.

  • Regular reports and recommendations
  • Iterative performance improvements
  • Quick technical support
Output: sustainable growth
